Digital Bussiness Conference

The first digital business Conference in Iraq will be held in collaboration with the Directorate of Culture and Youth of Sulaymaniyah. With the participation of experts in the field of digital business, digital marketing, and digital arts. The aim of this conference is to identify the importance of starting digital enterprise, providing career possibilities along with enhancing the inventive abilities among the youth and business owners.

The world of digital business with all its manifestations are at the core of most businesses. This conference will be hosted in order to raise awareness among project owners and the youth about the importance of starting digital enterprise and creating career possibilities for others. Furthermore, to keep up with the recent technologies and innovations occurring contemporary in the digital markets and to facilitate online marketing globally. Additionally, it is of importance for us as Kurds to harness and be adaptive to the developments occurring in the world of technology.
